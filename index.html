🚀 About Sohi Studios

Building Apps That Make a Difference

Sohi Studios is an independent Android app development studio with a clear mission: create simple, powerful, and user-friendly applications.

We believe great apps don't need to be complicated. Each app we publish on the Google Play Store is designed with care — clean interfaces, essential features, and no unnecessary bloat. Whether it's helping communities find lost items or optimizing your photos, every app serves a real purpose.

💡 Our Values

Why Sohi Studios?

The principles that guide every app we build

Simple Design

Clean, minimal interfaces free from clutter. Every button, every screen serves a clear purpose.

Privacy Focused
